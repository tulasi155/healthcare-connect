Government Health Schemes

🏥 Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)

Provides cashless healthcare coverage for eligible families at empanelled hospitals.


👩 Janani Suraksha Yojana

Supports safe motherhood by encouraging institutional deliveries for pregnant women.


💉 Universal Immunization Programme

Provides free vaccination for children and pregnant women across India.


Scheme information is for educational purposes. Please verify eligibility and benefits through official government sources.
