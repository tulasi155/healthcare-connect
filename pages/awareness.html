Health Awareness

🥗 Healthy Diet

Eat fresh fruits, vegetables, whole grains and drink enough water every day.


🏃 Daily Exercise

Exercise for at least 30 minutes daily to maintain good physical and mental health.


😴 Proper Sleep

Adults should sleep 7–8 hours every night for better immunity and concentration.


💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink 2–3 litres of water every day to keep your body hydrated.


🩺 Regular Health Check-up

Visit your doctor regularly for preventive health check-ups.


These health tips are for general awareness and healthy living.
